🌟 More Success Stories

Sundari Akka – The Seafood Queen 🌊

Once a humble tiffin seller, now the heart of Marina’s seafood scene. Marina’s food scene isn’t complete without Sundari Akka’s soulful seafood. Her fiery masalas and fearless spirit nourish both guests and dreams.

Sarala & Sandhiya – Chennai’s Trailblazing Trans-Auto Heroes 🛺

Once sidelined by society, Transwomen auto-rickshaw drivers in Chennai, now formalized with a ₹1 lakh government subsidy, earning dignity and independence on the roads .

K Kamalathal – The ₹1 Idli Paati 🍽️

She has become the soul of Vadivelampalayam’s breakfast culture. Her devotion to one-rupee idlis reflects warmth, dignity, and endless generosity. At 85, she continues to rise before dawn, cooking with love to serve the working poor a meal they can afford.

Sharmila – Driving Change in Coimbatore 🚌👩‍✈️

Breaking stereotypes behind the wheel, Sharmila is one of the few female bus drivers in Coimbatore. With determination in her heart and courage in her hands, she navigates the city's roads with pride. Her journey inspires countless women to take the driver's seat—literally and in life!

Uzhamagal – Threads of Change 👗

From a small tailoring corner to a thriving boutique in Coimbatore, Uzhamagal stitched her dreams with courage. Her boutique is now a symbol of empowerment, elegance, and entrepreneurship. She now mentors aspiring women tailors, proving that success grows stronger when shared.

Selvi – Beauty, Skill & Grit 💪💄

From Pondicherry’s local Self Help Group to training over 100 women, Selvi wears many hats — beautician, tailoring expert, and trainer. Fiercely independent, she stitched 13,000+ masks during COVID and now dreams of launching an online course. With just 10th-grade education and a Google Translate tab open, she’s proof that growth needs grit, not grammar.

Hema – The Hustling Powerhouse of Kanchipuram 🐝💼

Owner of an event management biz, beauty salon, and organic store, Hema is unstoppable. When COVID shut her salon, she pivoted to home physiotherapy, natural skincare, and neem-aloe sanitizers. She now plans to expand into herbal foods and tailoring supply wholesale — showing us that when the going gets tough, Hema gets going!

Sampurna Ganguly – Crafting Elegance, Empowering Women 🎨🌿

Founder of Silkykraftz, Sampurna blends heritage with sustainability through handcrafted leather and silk pieces made by women artisans in Shantiniketan. An engineer-turned-entrepreneur, she champions eco-friendly luxury that’s earned global recognition — proving style and social impact can go hand in hand.

Kalpana Saroj – Dalit Entrepreneur 💼

Once a child bride and daily wage worker, Kalpana rose to become the CEO of Kamani Tubes. Honored with the Padma Shri, she’s now a symbol of strength, resilience, and Dalit entrepreneurship in India.

Priya Malik – Wrestling Wonder of India 🏅

Priya Malik made India proud by clinching multiple wrestling medals on the global stage. Her fierce spirit and dedication have inspired countless girls to step onto the wrestling mat and fight for their dreams.

Dhee – Voice of Modern Tamil Nadu 🎶

A breakout indie artist known for "Enjoy Enjaami", Dhee brought Tamil music to global ears, blending tradition and rebellion with her soulful voice.

Sindhuja Rajaraman – India’s Youngest CEO 🖥️🚀

At just 14, Sindhuja Rajaraman became the CEO of Seppan, a Chennai-based animation company. From a child prodigy to a successful entrepreneur, she continues to inspire young minds across the country with her creativity, confidence, and leadership.

Avani Chaturvedi – India’s Sky Warrior ✈️💪

Avani Chaturvedi made history as one of the first female fighter pilots of the Indian Air Force. Breaking through the clouds—and barriers—she became the first Indian woman to fly a fighter jet solo. A true trailblazer inspiring girls to aim for the skies!

Kiran Mazumdar-Shaw – Biotech Billionaire 💊

Of Tamil origin, she founded Biocon and became India’s richest self-made woman. Her success in biotechnology has helped revolutionize healthcare in India.

Thirush Kamini – Century Maker for India 🏏

A record-setter and the first Indian woman to hit a World Cup century, Kamini is one of Tamil Nadu’s brightest cricket stars.

Sushma Sylendra Babu – Green Warrior 🌱

From awareness campaigns to green living, Sushma champions sustainable lifestyles and youth leadership, carrying forward her father's legacy of service.

S. Anitha – Symbol of Educational Equality 🎓

A Dalit student from Ariyalur who challenged NEET in court. Though she’s no longer with us, Anitha's story sparked a nationwide movement for equal education.

Ritu Karidhal – Rocket Woman of India 🚀

Though originally from Lucknow, she worked closely with ISRO’s space missions in Chennai. She played a crucial role in Chandrayaan and Mars missions, proving women belong in space too!

Isaivani – Breaking Barriers with Gaana 🎙️

A revolutionary female gaana singer from North Chennai, Isaivani challenged caste and gender norms in the male-dominated music scene. She was honored by BBC in their 100 Women list.

Sheela Kochouseph – Saree Queen of the South 👗

Though born in Kerala, Sheela has deep ties with Tamil Nadu through her successful brand V-Star and her work empowering rural women through textile jobs.

V. Nanammal – 99-Year-Old Yoga Queen 🧘‍♀️

From Coimbatore, she taught yoga to over 1 million people. Even in her 90s, she stayed strong and flexible, inspiring people to live healthy.

Sangeetha – Woman Bike Rider in Chennai 🚲

She rides for Rapido and Zomato in Chennai. After her husband's death, she took charge of her life and supports her family with pride.

Lady Kash – Tamil Rap Queen 🎤

A bold rapper who sings in Tamil and English. She works with big stars and uses her voice to speak for women and the youth.

Sudha Kongara – Director of Soorarai Pottru 🎬

She directed hit movies like *Soorarai Pottru*. Her films show strong characters and real stories, earning awards across the world.

Bharathi – Tamil Nadu’s First Woman Bus Driver 🚌

Bharathi made history by becoming the first woman to drive a TNSTC bus. She proved that roads are for everyone, not just men.

Rajeswari – Organic Farmer from Theni 🌾

She left a city job and became a farmer. Now she grows healthy, chemical-free food and trains other women to do the same.

Rajalakshmi – Pickle Seller Turned Entrepreneur 🥒

Thenmozhi – Artist & Voice for Justice 🎨✊
