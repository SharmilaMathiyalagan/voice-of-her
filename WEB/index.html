Voice Of Her

She believed she could, so she did.

💪 Who we are

Voice Of Her is a movement that shines a light on the unsung heroes — women who break boundaries by working in fields like street vending, transport, and delivery services.

“She believed she could, so she did.”

We aim to inspire, educate, and empower women through stories, support, and shared strength.

Join our mission to build a world where every woman is recognized, respected, and uplifted — no matter where she works. 🚀

🌟 Success Stories

Sundari Akka – The Seafood Queen 🌊

Once a humble tiffin seller, now the heart of Marina’s seafood scene. Marina’s food scene isn’t complete without Sundari Akka’s soulful seafood. Her fiery masalas and fearless spirit nourish both guests and dreams.

Sarala & Sandhiya – Chennai’s Trailblazing Trans-Auto Heroes 🛺

Once sidelined by society, Transwomen auto-rickshaw drivers in Chennai, now formalized with a ₹1 lakh government subsidy, earning dignity and independence on the roads .

K Kamalathal – The ₹1 Idli Paati 🍽️

She has become the soul of Vadivelampalayam’s breakfast culture. Her devotion to one-rupee idlis reflects warmth, dignity, and endless generosity. She serve the working poor a meal they can afford.
